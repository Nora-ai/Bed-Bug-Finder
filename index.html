Bed Bug Finder

Reported...but was it infested?

Bed bugs are a growing problem in New York City.The bed bug finder will help you spot and get a better view of infestations in NYC neighoborhoods.


New laws state that all property owners must report annually on their infestation history, even if there are no breakouts. All State supervised rental and mutual housing companies in NYC are required to provide new residential tenants with a completed copy of their filings.
